Comet Kits

Affordable robotics kits for education, featuring custom PCB, 3D printed chassis, and catered software development environment.

What is Comet Kits?

Comet Kits is an affordable robotics kit designed to give students access to STEM education through hands-on learning tools.

Our Solution

We designed custom PCBs, 3D printed chassis, and a dedicated curriculum to make robotics kits accessible to all students.
